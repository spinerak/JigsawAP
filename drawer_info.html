Piece drawer

What is the drawer?

  • You can store pieces in the drawer so they don’t show on the board.
  • Useful when you want to avoid moving large stacks of pieces by hand.

Good to know

  • The drawer only works for pieces that have never been moved manually.
  • Drawer actions are local only. Other players don’t see them, and they reset on refresh.

How does it work?

  • Pieces stored shows how many pieces are currently in the drawer.
  • Use # pieces to choose how many to move (1, 10, 100, or All).
  • Withdraw to drawer: Move to drawer / Move all to drawer — removes pieces from the board into the drawer.
  • Deposit from drawer: set Place at, then Move to board — returns pieces from the drawer to the board in the chosen area.
